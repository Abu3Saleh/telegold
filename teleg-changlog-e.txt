New in version 4.40

• Shareable chat folders: you can share up to 200 chats using a single invite link.
• Custom wallpapers: set an individual background for any chat between two people. Your chat partner can apply the same wallpaper or choose their own.
• Better bots: launch web applications from any chat. Web applications in groups support collaboration and multiplayer features.
• Other improvements: better video downloads and playback, improved SD card and Bluetooth headset support.
